About Our University

Founded in 1920, our university is a leader in higher education, committed to excellence in teaching, research, and service. We provide a supportive environment for students from all over the world.

Our Rich History

Started as a small technical institute in south-east London, we evolved into a full-fledged University in 1955. Our Main Library, founded in 1925, now houses over 50,000 physical volumes and millions of digital resources.

Research Infrastructure

We boast 15 specialized research centers. Our "Tesla Lab" is dedicated to renewable energy, while the "Turing Hub" focuses on advanced artificial intelligence and cyber security research.
